Hello , my name is Brian Ahrens. I am an IT major and I am taking HTML and CSS. My inspiration to make a bird feeding website came from my dad. I was looking outside one day and I thought with free images and simple content, that I could make a website about bird feeding. During the spring 2017 semester, I encountered a few issues with the bird feeders outside my home. First the weather was cold. Second some uninvited guests were in the backyard. The unnamed guests names are Mr.Squrriel and Mr. Kitty Cat. Mr. Squirrel tends to steal the bird's food, while Mr. Kitty Cat scares them. I created this webpage in order to resolve some of these issues. Thank you for checking my site out.
